“DeBenedictis is a working person’s musician, singing songs that are born amid the hard-scrabble hustle of everyday life.”

-Worcester Telegram

Mike DeBenedictis is a singer/songwriter living, eating burritos, and drinking rye whiskey in Boston, MA.

He has toured the Northeast, self-released a couple of albums, and played all over the Boston area. His songwriting style has been compared to that of Paul Westerberg (of The Replacements), but he draws influence from artists as disparate as Tom Waits and Bjork.

Expect new music from him in the near future, and buy him a beer or three next time you see him live.
